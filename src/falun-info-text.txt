Falun is a city, in Dalarna, that lies deep in the heart of Sweden. It is also a World Heritage Site! Known for its famous copper mine, Falu Koppargruva, that already saw production in the 11th century marks it as one of Swedens oldest cities that is still populated and active. Surrounded by the beautiful nature that Dalarna provides it is often the site for Winter Sports with its many facilities at the large sport complex, Lugnet. There lies the famous Lugnet Ski Jump Hill that will be one of the many sites for the olympic sports. We hope Falun will be one of your destinations here in Sweden for the Winter Olympic Games 2024!
